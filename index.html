About Us

A brief look at how Kites-R-Us came to be the empire it is today.

  • 1996

    "A Child is Born"

    04/06/1996. David O'Brien, founder of Kites-R-Us is born. This would turn out to be the day that changed the kite industry permanently. Born into a pro-Kite family, David was exposed to kites at a young age. Records show he flew his first kite at a staggering age of 3 months.

  • September 2017

    An Empire is Forged.

    Kites-R-Us is launched. David spotted a gap in the market for online kite sales and used his entrepreneurial skills to exploit this niche. Within minutes the world knew about Kites-R-Us. What looked like an overnight miracle to some, was years and years of planning and researching for David.

  • November 2017

    At the top

    Only two months after its conception, Kites-R-Us goes public. Mr. O'Brien, the now multi-award winning entrepreneur and Nobel Prize nominee has built an empire. Some critics say the quick rise to fame would be detrimental to the company's survival. These allegations were quickly dismissed by Mr. O'Brien who is quoted saying "Pressure makes Diamonds"
